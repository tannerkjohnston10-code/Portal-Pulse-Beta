# PP-DOS Component Catalog

## IntelligenceCard

Summarizes a source-backed insight with confidence, evidence count, source count, timestamp, and Explain This action.

## EvidenceCard

Shows supporting and contradicting evidence, source labels, confidence, and review state.

## ConfidenceBadge

Labels confidence as high, medium, low, conflicting, or unknown. Must not rely on color alone.

## ExplainThisButton

Opens explanation details for scores, charts, predictions, recommendations, simulations, and AI insights.

## TimelineCard

Shows a dated sequence event with confidence, source count, and related entities.

## KnowledgeGraphCard

Summarizes relationships, strength, evidence, and graph context.

## TrendCard

Shows movement, delta, sparkline, confidence, and reason for change.

## HeatMapCard

Shows density, region, position, or team activity using accessible labels and legends.

## TransferCard

Shows player movement context, status, involved teams, confidence, and source summary.

## PredictionCard

Shows projection, probability, confidence, reasons for, reasons against, and disclaimer.

## SimulationCard

Shows before/after state, assumptions, risk, confidence, and projected impact.

## ComparisonCard

Compares players, teams, positions, conferences, or time windows.

## AIInsightCard

Shows AI-proposed or Core-approved insight with evidence, confidence, and review status.

## WorkspaceHeader

Identifies workspace, context, selected sport/team/user view, and primary actions.

## WorkspaceSidebar

Provides workspace-specific navigation while preserving shared PP-DOS styling.

## IntelligenceRibbon

Signature stream component for live-style updates and watchlist movement.

## MissionControlPanel

High-density dashboard panel for Command Center and enterprise views.
