# PP-DOS Core Principles

## 1. Intelligence Before Decoration

Every visual element must clarify meaning, priority, relationship, state, or action.

## 2. Motion Explains Change

Motion should show what moved, what changed, what became more important, or what needs attention.

## 3. One Click To Understanding

Users should be one interaction away from evidence, confidence, assumptions, and context.

## 4. Explain Everything

Scores, rankings, predictions, simulations, AI insights, and recommendations must support explanation.

## 5. Build Wonder

The product should feel unusually capable, but the wonder must come from clarity and intelligence, not decoration.

## 6. Information Should Feel Alive

Timelines, streams, maps, rankings, and dashboards should show movement, recency, and change.

## 7. Consistency Creates Trust

Confidence, source, review, prediction, simulation, and historical states must mean the same thing everywhere.

## 8. Accessibility Is Non-Negotiable

Keyboard navigation, focus states, contrast, reduced motion, and readable type are product requirements.

## 9. Mobile Is First-Class

Mobile layouts should be designed, not collapsed.

## 10. Enterprise Quality Everywhere

Every surface should feel reliable enough for a media desk, coaching staff, or executive dashboard.
