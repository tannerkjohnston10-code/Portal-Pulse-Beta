# Workspace Design

## Mission Control

Mission Control is the high-density dashboard pattern for Command Center and enterprise overview screens.

## Fan Workspace

Focused on clarity, speed, watchlists, rankings, market movement, player/team pages, and briefings.

## Coach Workspace

Focused on roster decisions, simulations, Transfer Fit, Scholarship IQ, private notes, and staff collaboration.

## GM Workspace

Focused on multi-year roster construction, strategy, Digital Twin, class balance, and conference comparison.

## Media Workspace

Focused on research, source trails, exportable summaries, timelines, and relationship context.

## Developer Workspace

Focused on APIs, schemas, examples, usage, webhooks, and integration trust rules.

## Admin Workspace

Focused on queues, conflicts, review state, source checks, duplicate resolution, audit logs, and review speed.
