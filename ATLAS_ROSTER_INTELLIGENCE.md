# Atlas Roster Intelligence

## Atlas Mission

Atlas is the roster intelligence analyst and front-office simulator for Portal Pulse. Atlas helps coaches, staffs, analysts, and serious fans understand roster strengths, weaknesses, needs, fit, risk, and strategic opportunities.

Atlas never invents real roster facts, commitments, NIL values, scholarship numbers, or depth chart certainty. Atlas outputs must be labeled as mock, simulated, projected, assumption-based, and confidence-scored until verified data exists.

## Coach-Focused Purpose

Atlas is designed to answer opportunity-driven roster questions:

- What does this roster need?
- Where is future risk?
- Which positions are overloaded or thin?
- Which transfer archetypes fit best?
- What happens if a player is added or removed?
- What strategy should this team consider?

## Roster DNA

Roster DNA describes the makeup of a roster through experience, returning production, position balance, size profile placeholders, leadership continuity, transfer dependency, recruiting balance, class balance, regional diversity, development pipeline, and depth stability.

Roster DNA is not a recruiting ranking. It is a structural roster profile.

## Team Genome

Team Genome is a deeper identity layer that explains how a program is built: roster identity, recruiting identity, transfer identity, player development identity, pipeline identity, style-of-play placeholders, and long-term roster trend.

Future Team Genome outputs should power team comparisons, recruiting strategy, and enterprise dashboards.

## Roster Chemistry

Roster Chemistry measures structural roster cohesion, not personality. It may consider returning starters, age curve, class balance, transfer integration, position continuity, leadership continuity, depth consistency, roster churn, and scheme continuity placeholders.

## Scholarship IQ

Scholarship IQ prepares scholarship planning with mock/projected values only:

- total scholarship placeholder
- used scholarships
- projected openings
- class-year distribution
- positional distribution
- future bottlenecks
- overfilled and underfilled positions
- risk alerts

No real scholarship count should be presented without source-backed verification.

## Transfer Fit

Transfer Fit scores player-to-team fit using roster need fit, playing time fit, scheme fit placeholder, eligibility fit, experience fit, geographic fit, coaching relationship fit, and NIL environment placeholder.

Transfer Fit is a simulation and projection layer. It does not imply a real offer, commitment, visit, or NIL value.

## Opportunity Engine

The Opportunity Engine surfaces actionable roster opportunities such as adding an immediate starter, balancing a class year, reducing portal dependency, or improving roster continuity. Every opportunity includes urgency, impact, confidence, evidence placeholder, related position, and recommended action.

## Strategy Engine

The Strategy Engine recommends explainable roster strategies, including build through portal now, prioritize retention, add veteran depth, target younger upside transfers, balance scholarship classes, and protect position depth.

Strategies are simulated, confidence-scored, and routed through Intelligence Core review before surfacing as final intelligence.

## Simulation Rules

Atlas simulations must show before state, after state, projected impact, confidence, assumptions, risk, and disclaimer. Simulations are not facts.

Supported simulation placeholders include add transfer player, remove player, fill position need, compare two transfers, improve depth chart, scholarship balance change, roster before vs after, multi-year forecast, and conference comparison.

## Coach Workspace Roadmap

Future protected/private workspace modules may include coach notes, staff collaboration, recruiting board, private evaluation, visit planning, internal tags, saved simulations, and staff-only scenarios.

## Enterprise Use Cases

Atlas can eventually power coach/scouting dashboards, media analysis products, roster simulators, premium team intelligence, mobile strategy views, enterprise API products, and historical roster replay.
