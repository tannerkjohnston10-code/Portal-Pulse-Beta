# Portal Pulse Roadmap

Portal Pulse is evolving from an NCAA Transfer Portal product into the Bloomberg Terminal of College Athletics. The roadmap now prioritizes a flagship Command Center before the next major vertical dashboard so the product has one canonical intelligence surface for transfer movement, AI briefings, market signals, team momentum, NIL movement, watchlists, search, and future personalization.

## Long-Term Product Tracks

- **Command Center:** live intelligence briefing, breaking feed, market movers, prediction movers, transfer map, team momentum, watchlists, global search, and personalization.
- **Transfer Market Exchange:** market-style intelligence for activity, momentum, demand, competition, confidence, position scarcity, conference activity, watchlist movement, and source activity.
- **Atlas Team Builder:** Roster DNA, Team Genome, Scholarship IQ, Transfer Fit, opportunity engine, strategy engine, simulations, forecasts, and coach-facing workflow.
- **Player Intelligence:** player dashboards, Portal Pulse Score, timeline intelligence, prediction context, NIL intelligence, source-backed updates, and relationship graph.
- **Team Intelligence:** roster impact, portal gains and losses, depth chart pressure, position needs, coaching context, and conference comparison.
- **Trust Infrastructure:** source registry, claim verification, confidence scoring, conflict detection, admin review, audit logs, and historical snapshots.
- **Athena Intelligence Network:** specialized analyst agents, daily briefings, proposal review, safety gates, Core approval, and future conversational intelligence.
- **Premium Intelligence:** alerts, private watchlists, morning briefings, roster simulators, relationship graph, advanced analytics, API access, and mobile surfaces.
- **Founder Book:** master company blueprint for vision, product, architecture, business units, customers, revenue, IP language, and long-term roadmap.
- **Product Bible:** master product specification for products, features, workflows, screens, agents, personas, APIs, design expectations, and future product decisions.
- **Product Specifications:** complete module specs for Command Center, Player Intelligence, Team Intelligence, Rankings, Athena agents, Knowledge Graph, Historical Replay, TMX, Atlas, API Platform, and Developer Platform.
- **Design Operating System:** PP-DOS visual language, semantic tokens, workspace layouts, motion, accessibility, Intelligence Stream, Explain This, and theme architecture.
- **Technical Architecture Bible:** master engineering architecture blueprint for data flow, Intelligence Core, Platform SDK, APIs, AI agents, infrastructure, security, testing, scalability, and future expansion.
- **Engineering Standards Manual:** mandatory engineering playbook for repository standards, coding, naming, architecture rules, UI, AI, API, testing, security, performance, PRs, Definition of Done, and culture.
- **Portal Pulse Labs:** simulation and scenario intelligence workspace for mock what-if analysis, separated from verified reporting and gated by Intelligence Core principles.
- **Program Intelligence Workspace:** unified school/program workspace powered internally by the Digital Twin Engine with verified, analytical, historical, prediction, and simulation sections clearly separated.
- **GM Workspace:** executive front-office intelligence for roster health, scholarship allocation, transfer portfolio strategy, recruiting pipeline, organizational planning, AI Executive Council recommendations, investment boards, and reports.
- **Multi-Sport Foundation:** Sport Engine, universal athlete/program entities, sport-scoped Knowledge Graph, multi-sport AI coverage, search filters, and shared sport switching for football, men's basketball, women's basketball, baseball, and softball.
- **Portal Pulse Network:** professional collaboration layer for profiles, reputation, intelligence feeds, structured reports, shared watchlists, communities, evidence-first discussion, Knowledge Exchange, moderation placeholders, and future enterprise collaboration.
- **Production Foundation:** authentication framework, centralized permissions, organizations, API gateway, event expansion, job queue, search infrastructure, caching policy, observability, feature flags, deployment, backup, recovery, and enterprise readiness.
- **Real-Time Intelligence Pipeline:** compliant source intake, real-time claim candidates, evidence attachment, conflict scanning, Intelligence Core review triggers, Knowledge Graph proposals, AI agent notifications, dashboard distribution hooks, alert queues, and monitoring snapshots.
- **Portal Pulse Experience:** internal beta polish layer with Demo Mode, feedback widget, walkthrough, homepage refinement, navigation improvements, Coach Workspace entry point, Beta Ops metrics, beta checklist, and UX standards documentation.
