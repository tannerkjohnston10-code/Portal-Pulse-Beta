# Customer Overview

## Fans

- **Pain Points:** Fragmented news, rumors, unclear confidence, hard-to-follow roster movement.
- **Portal Pulse Solution:** Command Center, player/team intelligence, rankings, TMX, maps, replay.
- **Willingness-To-Pay Hypothesis:** Moderate for premium alerts, watchlists, and deeper dashboards.
- **Likely Product Path:** Free tier to fan subscription.

## Super Fans

- **Pain Points:** Need deeper context, historical movement, prediction changes, team comparisons.
- **Portal Pulse Solution:** Premium dashboards, saved views, watchlists, Athena briefings.
- **Willingness-To-Pay Hypothesis:** Higher than casual fans for year-round intelligence.
- **Likely Product Path:** Premium fan tier.

## Media

- **Pain Points:** Time-consuming research, fragmented source trails, fast news cycles.
- **Portal Pulse Solution:** Research dashboards, source timelines, historical replay, rankings, API snippets.
- **Willingness-To-Pay Hypothesis:** Moderate to high for workflow speed.
- **Likely Product Path:** Media/research tier.

## Podcasts

- **Pain Points:** Need quick storylines, confident context, trend explanations.
- **Portal Pulse Solution:** Briefings, TMX, player/team summaries, quote-safe source summaries.
- **Willingness-To-Pay Hypothesis:** Moderate for content acceleration.
- **Likely Product Path:** Creator subscription or media tier.

## Coaches

- **Pain Points:** Roster needs, transfer fit, scholarship planning, depth risk, decision support.
- **Portal Pulse Solution:** Atlas, Coach Workspace, roster simulator, Transfer Fit, strategy engine.
- **Willingness-To-Pay Hypothesis:** High if private, accurate, secure, and workflow-integrated.
- **Likely Product Path:** Team subscription.

## Recruiting Departments

- **Pain Points:** Relationship tracking, recruiting boards, transfer targets, class balance.
- **Portal Pulse Solution:** Knowledge Graph, Coach Workspace, recruiting board, Atlas, source tracking.
- **Willingness-To-Pay Hypothesis:** High for professional workflow tooling.
- **Likely Product Path:** Portal Pulse Pro.

## Athletic Directors

- **Pain Points:** Program oversight, roster health, market context, department reporting.
- **Portal Pulse Solution:** Enterprise dashboards, audit logs, team intelligence, historical views.
- **Willingness-To-Pay Hypothesis:** High for trusted reporting and strategic insight.
- **Likely Product Path:** Athletic department enterprise tier.

## NIL Collectives

- **Pain Points:** Need careful market context without exposing private data or overstating values.
- **Portal Pulse Solution:** NIL intelligence labels, confidence disclaimers, market context, source summaries.
- **Willingness-To-Pay Hypothesis:** Moderate to high if compliance-aware.
- **Likely Product Path:** Enterprise or partner plan.

## Developers

- **Pain Points:** No clean, confidence-aware college athletics intelligence API.
- **Portal Pulse Solution:** Versioned API, webhooks, data exports, source-aware objects.
- **Willingness-To-Pay Hypothesis:** Usage-based, tiered by access and volume.
- **Likely Product Path:** API tier.

## Enterprise Partners

- **Pain Points:** Need reliable data feeds, widgets, historical datasets, and reporting products.
- **Portal Pulse Solution:** Portal Pulse Data, white-label widgets, historical exports, partner APIs.
- **Willingness-To-Pay Hypothesis:** High for licensed data and integration.
- **Likely Product Path:** Enterprise data contracts.
