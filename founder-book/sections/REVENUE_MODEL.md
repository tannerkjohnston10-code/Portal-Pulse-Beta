# Revenue Model

## Free Tier

Public Command Center previews, selected player/team pages, limited rankings, and basic news-style intelligence. Goal: brand trust, audience growth, and discovery.

## Fan Subscription

Deeper watchlists, alerts, saved views, full TMX access, historical replay, player/team dashboards, and premium rankings.

## Premium Fan Tier

Advanced dashboards, Athena briefings, comparison tools, custom alerts, premium maps, and expanded historical analysis.

## Media / Research Tier

Research workflows, source timelines, exportable summaries, historical replay, rankings methodology, and faster content production tools.

## Coach / Team Subscription

Atlas, Coach Workspace, roster simulator, recruiting board, scholarship planning, Transfer Fit, strategy tools, and private saved scenarios.

## Athletic Department Enterprise Tier

Department-wide dashboards, role-based access, audit logs, program oversight, historical reporting, enterprise support, and integrations.

## API Tier

Versioned endpoints, structured intelligence objects, search, rankings, historical data, webhooks, and usage-based pricing.

## Data Licensing

Historical datasets, structured feeds, partner analytics, and enterprise reporting.

## White-Label Widgets

Embeddable rankings, player cards, team momentum, TMX widgets, maps, and source-aware intelligence modules.

## Mobile Premium

Push alerts, watchlists, morning briefings, saved views, offline-adjacent reading, and personalized mobile intelligence.

## Future Marketplace

Potential marketplace for premium data modules, partner integrations, analyst packages, team templates, and enterprise add-ons.
