# Volume II — The Product

## Portal Pulse OS

Portal Pulse OS is the shared intelligence operating system underneath every product surface. It includes the Intelligence Core, Platform SDK, source/claim systems, Knowledge Graph, Athena agents, historical replay, and API foundation.

## Command Center

Command Center is the flagship dashboard. It should answer what happened, what changed, why it matters, how confident Portal Pulse is, and what users should explore next.

Current status: built in mock/demo form.

## Player Intelligence Center

The Player Intelligence Center turns each player page into a premium intelligence dashboard with profile context, transfer timeline, predictions, NIL placeholders, source intelligence, relationship graph, and historical replay hooks.

Current status: built in mock/demo form.

## Team Intelligence Center

The Team Intelligence Center is a program operating dashboard for roster strengths, needs, portal class, timeline, coaching context, AI storylines, recruiting pipeline, and comparisons.

Current status: built in mock/demo form.

## Rankings Intelligence Platform

Rankings are treated as explainable intelligence products, not popularity lists. Future rankings should preserve history, confidence, methodology, and factor explanations.

Current status: built in mock/demo form.

## Transfer Market Exchange

Transfer Market Exchange, or TMX, uses market-style language to explain activity, attention, momentum, demand, competition, confidence, position scarcity, and source volume. This is an analytical metaphor only and must never imply athletes are financial assets.

Current status: built in mock/demo form.

## Athena Intelligence Network

Athena is the Chief Intelligence Officer. Agents may propose findings but cannot publish directly. The Intelligence Core owns verification, confidence, conflict checks, and surfacing decisions.

Current status: architecture and mock agent outputs are prepared.

## Knowledge Graph

The Knowledge Graph connects players, teams, schools, coaches, sources, claims, visits, predictions, timelines, regions, and relationships.

Current status: architecture and mock visualization placeholders are prepared.

## Historical Replay

Historical Replay preserves what Portal Pulse knew, when it knew it, how confident it was, and what changed later.

Current status: Apollo mock replay foundation is built.

## Spatial Intelligence

Spatial Intelligence powers maps, transfer flows, recruiting pipelines, regional trends, and future geographic analytics.

Current status: Cartographer mock map foundation is built.

## Atlas Roster Intelligence

Atlas supports Roster DNA, Team Genome, Roster Chemistry, Scholarship IQ, Transfer Fit, opportunity engine, strategy engine, simulations, and forecasts.

Current status: Team Builder mock workspace is built.

## Portal Pulse Labs

Portal Pulse Labs is a future innovation layer for experimental models, research tools, new sports, product prototypes, and strategic partner pilots.

Current status: future vision.

## Digital Twin

Digital Twin is a future simulation layer for teams, rosters, conferences, recruiting classes, and multi-year scenarios.

Current status: future vision.

## Coach Workspace

Coach Workspace is a future protected product for notes, staff collaboration, recruiting board, visit planning, private evaluations, and team-specific strategy.

Current status: placeholder preview only.

## GM Workspace

GM Workspace is a future personnel department operating layer for roster construction, transfer strategy, scholarship planning, and multi-year team building.

Current status: future vision.
