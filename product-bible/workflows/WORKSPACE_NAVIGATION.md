# Workspace Navigation

Portal Pulse OS should be organized around workspaces rather than isolated pages.

## Philosophy

Users do not come to Portal Pulse for pages. They come to accomplish jobs:

- understand what changed
- monitor a team
- evaluate a player
- prepare a story
- plan a roster
- review a claim
- build with data

Navigation should group products around those jobs.

- Command Center
- Player Intelligence
- Team Intelligence
- Rankings
- TMX
- Maps
- Watchlists
- Historical Replay
