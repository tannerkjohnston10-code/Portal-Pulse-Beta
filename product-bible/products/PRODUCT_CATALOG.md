# Product Catalog

This catalog is the product index for Portal Pulse. Complete module specifications now live in `product-bible/products/specs/`, and the master linked catalog lives in `product-bible/products/MASTER_PRODUCT_CATALOG.md`.

Status note: most product surfaces are **In Development** because mock/demo foundations, services, routes, or documentation exist. That does not mean production data, real-time operations, or enterprise workflows are complete.

## Command Center

- **Status:** Built in mock/demo form.
- **Purpose:** Flagship intelligence dashboard for what matters right now.
- **Primary Users:** Fans, media, analysts, power users.
- **Core Value:** AI briefing, breaking feed, market movers, team momentum, prediction movement, watchlists, and maps in one place.
- **Future Roadmap:** Personalization, saved layouts, real-time updates, premium modules.
- **Full Spec:** `product-bible/products/specs/COMMAND_CENTER_SPEC.md`
