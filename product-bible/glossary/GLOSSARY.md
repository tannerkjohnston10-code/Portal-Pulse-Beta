# Product Glossary

## Claim

A reported assertion that may be true, false, partial, stale, or conflicting. A claim is not automatically a fact.

## Fact

A verified, source-backed item that has met the appropriate review standard.

## Prediction

A projection based on signals. Predictions are never facts.

## Confidence

The platform's labeled certainty level based on evidence, source reliability, conflicts, and recency.

## Intelligence

Explained significance derived from evidence, claims, history, context, and reasoning.

## Workspace

A job-oriented product area designed around user workflows rather than isolated pages.

## Mock

Fictional example data used for architecture and product design.

## Simulation

An assumption-based scenario used for planning or exploration. It is not a real claim.

## Source

A public or approved record that supports claims or context.

## Intelligence Core

The trust layer responsible for evidence review, confidence scoring, conflict checks, explanations, and surfacing decisions.
