# User Personas

## Casual Fan

- **Goals:** Understand major portal movement quickly.
- **Pain Points:** Too many scattered updates and unclear rumors.
- **Daily Workflow:** Checks scores, team news, social media, and occasional transfer updates.
- **Portal Pulse Value:** Simple, trusted summaries with confidence labels and next-click context.

## Power User

- **Goals:** Track many players, teams, rankings, and market changes.
- **Pain Points:** Needs depth, speed, and alerts without living across many tabs.
- **Daily Workflow:** Checks Command Center, watchlists, rankings, TMX, player pages.
- **Portal Pulse Value:** Premium dashboards, saved views, historical replay, Athena briefings.

## Coach

- **Goals:** Improve roster decisions and evaluate transfer fit.
- **Pain Points:** Roster risk, scholarship pressure, depth uncertainty, time constraints.
- **Daily Workflow:** Reviews needs, targets, depth, visits, roster scenarios.
- **Portal Pulse Value:** Atlas, Transfer Fit, Scholarship IQ, simulations, Coach Workspace.

## Recruiting Coordinator

- **Goals:** Organize targets, relationships, visits, and class balance.
- **Pain Points:** Fragmented recruiting information and manual board upkeep.
- **Daily Workflow:** Tracks prospects, coaches, visits, communication, roster needs.
- **Portal Pulse Value:** Knowledge Graph, recruiting board, relationship intelligence, source timelines.

## General Manager

- **Goals:** Build sustainable roster strategy.
- **Pain Points:** Multi-year planning, portal dependency, scholarship allocation, position balance.
- **Daily Workflow:** Reviews roster health, transfer targets, class distribution, strategy scenarios.
- **Portal Pulse Value:** GM Workspace, Digital Twin, Atlas, multi-year forecasts.

## Athletic Director

- **Goals:** Understand program health and strategic risk.
- **Pain Points:** Needs trusted dashboards without drowning in details.
- **Daily Workflow:** Reviews reports, program momentum, roster risk, department priorities.
- **Portal Pulse Value:** Enterprise dashboards, auditability, program-level intelligence.

## Media Reporter

- **Goals:** Report accurately and quickly.
- **Pain Points:** Source verification, timeline reconstruction, context, conflicting reports.
- **Daily Workflow:** Tracks breaking movement, sources, player/team context, storylines.
- **Portal Pulse Value:** Source-backed summaries, timelines, history, relationship context.

## Podcast Host

- **Goals:** Prepare strong segments and explain storylines.
- **Pain Points:** Time-consuming research and uncertain claims.
- **Daily Workflow:** Builds show notes from news, team trends, player movement, rankings.
- **Portal Pulse Value:** Briefings, TMX, rankings, storylines, watchlists.

## Research Analyst

- **Goals:** Study trends and historical movement.
- **Pain Points:** Lack of structured historical data and confidence metadata.
- **Daily Workflow:** Pulls datasets, compares trends, studies conferences and positions.
- **Portal Pulse Value:** Historical datasets, APIs, replay, exports, confidence-aware records.

## Developer

- **Goals:** Build products with safe college athletics intelligence.
- **Pain Points:** No clean, versioned, source-aware API.
- **Daily Workflow:** Consumes endpoints, tests responses, builds integrations.
- **Portal Pulse Value:** API Platform, docs, webhooks, stable contracts.

## Enterprise Partner

- **Goals:** Integrate trusted intelligence into external products.
- **Pain Points:** Needs reliable data, rights-safe summaries, enterprise support.
- **Daily Workflow:** Consumes feeds, embeds widgets, reviews reporting.
- **Portal Pulse Value:** Portal Pulse Data, white-label widgets, APIs, datasets.
